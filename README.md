## What is Genatio?

Genatio is a trustless grant app for open source builders. Every project submission is verified autonomously by GenLayer Intelligent Contracts through Optimistic Democracy — five independent validators reach consensus on-chain. No human approves or rejects anything. The AI and the chain decide everything.

> "We can't touch your money. We can't approve or reject your project. The Intelligent Contract and the chain decide everything."

---

## How It Works

### 01 · Submit
Connect your wallet and submit your open source project — GitHub URL, project story, funding goal, and campaign duration.

### 02 · Verify
A GenLayer Intelligent Contract autonomously fetches your GitHub data, evaluates your project across several factors, and scores it through Optimistic Democracy consensus. Projects scoring 40+ go live immediately.

### 03 · Fund
The community funds active projects directly. Donations are tracked on-chain. The community can flag suspicious projects — a dispute contract re-investigates using the same AI verification.
